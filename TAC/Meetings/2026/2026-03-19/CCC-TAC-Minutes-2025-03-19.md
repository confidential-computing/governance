## New Business
DM shared the list of the open PRs and requested the TAC members to contribute to the open PRs so that they can be successfully archived. 

DM announced that the team had completed a board meeting on March 18, 2026 where they reviewed ISO discussions and TAC engagement, and assigned co-chairs the responsibility of reviewing previous meeting minutes to help plan future sessions. DM encouraged the participants spending time reading and editing content for Blueprint B and C documents, with some comments already added by Rene and Mingshen.


The group discussed document B, with Nicolas Maeding (NM) suggesting it should highlight more community efforts rather than just cloud provider capabilities. MB and DM clarified that the goal was not to approve the document immediately but to gather feedback over a two-week period, with potential for further iterations. The team debated how to handle references to commercial and proprietary solutions, with Ofir raising questions about including cloud service provider technologies. Mike noted the need to consult with the Linux Foundation regarding rules for including commercial solutions in the document. The TAC members discussed feedback on two confidential computing blueprint documents (B and C).
